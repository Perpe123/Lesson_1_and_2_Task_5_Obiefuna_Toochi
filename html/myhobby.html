My Sweet Hobby

Everyone should have a hobby. Sketching and drawing can take my mind off anything that has been stressing me out. This is a hobby you can do at any time, it is not expensive. Sketching and drawing not only improve your creative skills but also help your aesthetic skills. These skills can be applied in a number of different areas in your life.

Sketching is beneficial for wellness and holistic health. As your sketches become better and better, your confidence and self esteem increases. I feel a sense of satisfaction and achievement on completing a sketch. Feeling satisfied and confident goes a long way in improving your wellness quotient. You can draw just anything but personally, I love to draw human beings especially the female gender just like the one below.

Human body is symmetric in nature and this was one of the challenges I faced while drawing as a child. Then, one hand or foot would always be bigger than the other, but now, I'd say I've become an expert in drawing. For me, drawing is everything and more. It's an amazing talent and a beautiful hobby. Basic drawing materials and their uses
Drawing material Use
Paper A surface on which the sketching is made
Graphite pencil To create different types of artwork
Sharpener To sharpen your pencil
Eraser To clean off mistakes as you draw
Ruler Helps to create straight lines and shapes more easily and to measure different things while drawing
Drawing compass It lets you draw circles or arcs on different materials like paper
